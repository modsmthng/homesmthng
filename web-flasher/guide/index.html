Power and battery safety

  • Always verify the voltage, connector, polarity and board revision yourself against the manufacturer’s current documentation before connecting power or a battery. HOMEsmthng cannot guarantee that the hardware information in this guide is complete or error-free.
  • Use a regulated 5 V USB supply. Its amp rating is available capacity; the display draws only what it needs.
  • For flashing, use a USB-C cable that carries data, not a charge-only cable.
  • Use only a protected single-cell 3.7 V LiPo on boards with a documented battery input.
  • Check connector polarity every time. A matching plug shape does not guarantee matching + and − wiring.
  • Keep the battery away from heat and sharp edges, and never use a swollen or damaged cell.
  • Do not overtighten enclosure screws; printed plastic and display boards can crack.

Step 2

Install HOMEsmthng

Use desktop Chrome or Edge, the exact display selection and a USB data cable.

  1. 1
    Connect the display

    Plug it directly into your computer and close any serial monitor using the port.

  2. 2
    Choose the exact model

    Select Update to keep settings, or Install / Reinstall to erase the display completely.

  3. 3
    Flash and restart

    Wait for “Finished”, then unplug and reconnect the USB cable once to restart.

  4. 4
    Join 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi

    Follow the first-setup information on the display, then pair HOMEsmthng with Apple Home.
